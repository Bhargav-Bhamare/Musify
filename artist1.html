About

Rahat Fateh Ali Khan is a Pakistani Singer,primarily Qawwali,a form of Sufi devotional music. Khan is one of the most popular and highest paid singers in Pakistan. He is the nephew of Nusrat Fateh Ali Khan,son of Farurukh Fateh Ali Khan and grandson of Qawwali singer Fateh Ali Khan.In addition to Qawwali, he also performs ghazals and other light music. He is also well-known as a playback singer in Hindi Cinema and the Pakistan film Industry. As of May 2025, Rahat Fateh Ali Khan has over 17.8 million monthly listeners on Spotify, reflecting his widespread popularity. His most streamed tracks include "O Re Piya," "Teri Ore," and "Ajj Din Chadheya," each garnering millions of plays. Rahat's ability to blend traditional Qawwali with contemporary music styles has earned him a global fanbase and solidified his position as a leading figure in South Asian music.
